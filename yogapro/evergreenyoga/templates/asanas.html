Types Of Yoga

HATH YOGA

Haṭha yoga is a branch of yoga which uses physical techniques to preserve and channel the vital force or energy. The Sanskrit word haṭha literally means "force" thus alluding to a system of physical techniques

BIKRAM YOGA

Bikram yoga is a cult-favorite style of hatha yoga that counts many celebrities as fans — but it's one of those workouts that you may want to research before heading off to a class. For one thing, don't expect your instructor to demonstrate the moves.

ASHTANGA YOGA

Ashtanga yoga is a cult-favorite style of hatha yoga that counts many celebrities as fans — but it's one of those workouts that you may want to research before heading off to a class. For one thing, don't expect your instructor to demonstrate the moves.

VINYASA YOGA

Vinyasa yoga also called "flow" because of the smooth way the poses run together, is one of the most popular contemporary styles of yoga. It's a broad classification that encompasses many different types of yoga, including Ashtanga and power yoga

POWER YOGA

Power yoga is a general term used to describe a vigorous, fitness-based approach to vinyasa-style yoga.Though many consider power yoga to be superficial "gym yoga," this style of yoga practice was originally closely modeled on the Ashtanga method
